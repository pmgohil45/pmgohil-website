FAQ
What subjects do you teach?
We cover all subjects for grades 8 to 12, with a focus on Science and Mathematics. Additionally, we offer tutoring for Engineering students, focusing on specialized subjects like M1, M2, M3, and Applied Mechanics.
What boards do you support?
We provide tutoring for all major boards, including CBSE, ICSE, SSC, and HSC.
How are online lessons conducted?
Online sessions are conducted via platforms like Zoom or Google Meet, providing the same quality of personalized education as home tutoring.
What is your cancellation policy?
Sessions can be rescheduled with 24-hour notice. In case of missed lessons without prior notification, the session will be considered forfeited.
Do you offer refunds?
Refunds are available for unused sessions if requested within 14 days of the lesson package purchase.
