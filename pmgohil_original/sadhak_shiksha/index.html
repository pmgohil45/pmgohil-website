Meet Professor Keyur Modi

(Guiding Students for Over 14 Years)

Professor Keyur Modi, an Engineering graduate from the E&TC branch of Savitribai Phule Pune University, has been passionately teaching students for over 14 years. Having worked in corporate roles, his heart led him to the world of education, where he's helped thousands of students excel academically and choose the right career paths.

At SadhakShiksha, we believe that education is not just about academics but about setting the foundation for a successful future. We don't make students match the teacher's pace; we tailor our teaching to match the student's learning style. Whether it's through home tutoring or online lessons, Professor Modi ensures each student receives personalized attention and care.

FAQ
What subjects do you teach?
We cover all subjects for grades 8 to 12, with a focus on Science and Mathematics. Additionally, we offer tutoring for Engineering students, focusing on specialized subjects like M1, M2, M3, and Applied Mechanics.
What boards do you support?
We provide tutoring for all major boards, including CBSE, ICSE, SSC, and HSC.
How are online lessons conducted?
Online sessions are conducted via platforms like Zoom or Google Meet, providing the same quality of personalized education as home tutoring.
What is your cancellation policy?
Sessions can be rescheduled with 24-hour notice. In case of missed lessons without prior notification, the session will be considered forfeited.
Do you offer refunds?
Refunds are available for unused sessions if requested within 14 days of the lesson package purchase.
